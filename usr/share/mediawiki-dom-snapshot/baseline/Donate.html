Help
For any questions regarding your donation contact our fundraising support .
Support Kicksecure in two ways: (1.) Purchase Plus Support . (2.) Contribute any amount in lieu of a donation - consultation will be negotiated accordingly ( contact us ). (Note A.) Businesses may be able to claim Plus Support as a tax-deductible expense - consult a tax advisor. (Note B.) Charitable donations are not supported or tax-deductible for any party.

Kicksecure is an amazing, secure-by-default, Open Source, and free operating system with the latest security research in place. But we need your help to keep it independent from corporate and government influence. CONSIDER DONATING TODAY to charge up our efforts and keep Kicksecure alive! -- The Kicksecure team

There are plenty of payment options to choose from. If you don't find your preferred method, get in contact !

New program ! Donors are now eligible to be added to a list of our donors. Find out more about our Donor Recognition Program .

If you can't donate but you have free time to spare, you can help us as well. There are many ways you can help us .
