Browser specific.

  • A) Most browser (such as Mozilla Firefox or Chrome): No special notice.
  • B) File:Iconfinder tor 386502.png Tor Browser:
    • If using Tor or Tor Browser, at time of writing PayPal was blocking connections from the Tor network.
    • PayPal very most likely requires use of a clearnet browser.
    • This is a decision by PayPal and unspecific to Kicksecure .
    • For alternatives, consider for example donate by bank wire or donate by crypto currency .
